Malo e lelei

An invitation from the TFW9s Tonga Juniors Subcommittee
For our junior players

Help our kids stand proud in who they are.

We are Tongan families raising our children through rugby, faith and culture as we build toward the 2026 Heritage Cup.

Close to 80 junior players across U7 to U12 are watching players like you carry our flag. To them, you are proof of what a Tongan kid from a family like theirs can become.
80Junior players
U7–U12Age groups
2026Heritage Cup

Option 1: Visit training

Drop in to one of our training sessions, even briefly, to meet the kids and say a few words.

Option 2: Send a video

If your schedule is full, a short encouragement video we can play for the teams would mean the world.

A few minutes of your time becomes a memory these children carry for life. It helps them feel loto lahi, stand proud in their Tongan identity, and understand that their heritage belongs on the field with them.

There is no pressure and no expectation. If it is a yes in any form, we will work around whatever suits you. If the timing is not right, we still want to say thank you for everything you carry for our people.

Faka’apa’apa atu,
TFW9s Tonga Juniors Subcommittee
